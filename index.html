MALAWI THE WARM HEART OF AFRICA

Malawi is a landlocked country which is found in central-southern africa.The president of Malawi country is His Excelency Dr Lazorus MCathy Chakwera and his vice is Dr Saulos Kraus Chilima.It has got beautiful lakes like,lake Malawi, lake Malombe and lake Chilwa which keeps fish like the great Chambo fish,Kampango,Mlamba and other fish.Apart from lakes, Malawi has nice national parks like Liwonde National Park,vwaza national park just to mention a few were animals like(the big five) lions,elephants,leopards,rhinos, buffalos and other wild animals are kept.
